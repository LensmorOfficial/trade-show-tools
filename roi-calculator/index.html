How We Calculate

ROI = (Revenue - Total Cost) / Total Cost x 100

Revenue = Leads x Conversion Rate x Avg Deal Size

Cost per Lead = Total Cost / Number of Leads

Break-even Leads = Total Cost / (Conversion Rate x Deal Size)

Industry benchmarks: B2B trade show conversion rates typically range 5-15%. Average cost per lead at trade shows is $100-$500, compared to $300-$1,000 for digital channels (CEIR Index Report).
